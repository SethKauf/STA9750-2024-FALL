defined as the highest ratio of Total Fares to Expenses?

USAGE_AND_FINANCIALS |> group_by(Agency,Mode) |> summarize(fbx_rcv = sum(Total Fares, na.rm = TRUE) / sum(Expenses, na.rm = TRUE), .groups=“drop”) |> arrange(desc(fbx_rcv)) |> slice_head(n=1) # Transit Authority of Central Kentucky – Vanpool
